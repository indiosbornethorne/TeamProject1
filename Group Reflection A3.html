Group Processes and Communications

Cinco group communicated through our Discord Server. This allowed us to communicate on an ongoing basis, as one channel acted as a message board for personal updates and discussion about the assignment, while another would act as a place to proof read other members work. There was communication between at least 2 of the members of the group every second day or so during the first weeks of the assignment and then everyone, everyday during the last week.

Our group was good at communicating downtime in advance and being aware of each-other’s scheduling issues and other commitments that had to come before the project.
